Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 26, 2026

1. Introduction

Reputa Score Protocol ("the Protocol", "we", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and safeguarding of data within the Pi Network ecosystem. By using the Protocol, you agree to the terms described herein.

2. Data Collection and Scope

We operate on the principle of data minimization. The Protocol analyzes publicly available blockchain data to provide reputation scoring. The data processed includes:

  • Public Wallet Addresses: Used to analyze transaction history and generate trust scores.
  • User Identifiers (UID): Essential for maintaining account status, VIP memberships, and access control.
  • Technical Metadata: Information such as device type and browser version to optimize interface performance and security.

3. Data Usage and Processing

Data collected is utilized strictly for the following purposes:

  • Reputation Analysis: Processing ledger data to calculate accuracy-based trust metrics.
  • Risk Mitigation: Identifying suspicious patterns to protect the community from fraudulent activities.
  • Service Optimization: Enhancing the algorithmic accuracy of our scoring engine.

4. Data Protection and Security

The Protocol employs industry-standard encryption and security measures. We maintain a non-custodial architecture, meaning we never request, store, or have access to your private keys or passphrases. Analysis is performed on public ledgers, ensuring no link is made between blockchain data and your real-world identity.

5. Third-Party Disclosure

Reputa Score Protocol maintains a strict no-sale policy. We do not sell, trade, or rent user data to third parties. Data remains within the secure infrastructure of the Protocol and is only used to facilitate the services requested by the user.

6. User Rights and Controls

Users maintain full autonomy over their data. You have the right to request the deletion of any personal identifiers stored in our database, inquire about the logic used for your specific wallet analysis, and manage your visibility settings through the application interface.

8. Contact Information

For formal inquiries regarding data privacy and security, please contact our support team through our official communication channels on Telegram.
